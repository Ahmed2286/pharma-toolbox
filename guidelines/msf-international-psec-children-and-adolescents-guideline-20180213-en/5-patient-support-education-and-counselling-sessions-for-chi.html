PSEC to caregivers
individually before

Partial disclosure to be
started approx. around
the age of 5-6:

Two sessions explaining
the visit to the clinic, the
immune system and the
red germ without naming
HIV.

Full disclosure to be
started approx. around
the age of 9-10. HIV
status to be disclosed
to all children>12 if not
previously done:

Session explaining that
the red germ is HIV and
ways of transmission &
prevention.

Post-test
counselling.

Emotional support in case
of HIV+ result.
Education & support on
how to stay negative (harm
reduction skills).

Entry into care
Initial assessment
of patient’s needs
understanding.

Explore educational,
emotional, social and
behavioral needs.

Follow-up for
patients not yet
eligible for ART

Pre-ART session.
Education on HIV & ART.
Counselling to ensure
clinical FU.

ART initiation and
early follow-up

HIV/ART education
session.

Education on HIV & ART.
Counselling on motivations
for Rx, support system,
appointments attendance
and readiness to start ART.

ART initiation
session.

Education & counselling on
Rx schedule, side effects,
Rx reminders, storing of Rx
and missed doses.

Adherence follow-
up counselling at
month 1 on ART.

Education: Assessment of
prior knowledge retention
and revision.
Counselling: assessment on
the implementation of the

Creation of new strategies

Communication between
caregiver and child.

Adherence follow-

Education on Viral load

Counselling to set

Adherence follow-

Revision of information
on viral load test and
treatment goals.
Information on the
differentiated models of
