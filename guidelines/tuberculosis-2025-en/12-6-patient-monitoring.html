12

Treatment of severe TB-IRIS is based on corticosteroids (except in the case of Kaposi’s sarcoma
or cryptococcal meningitis, for which corticosteroids are contraindicated).
Patients on corticosteroids should be monitored to detect any other opportunistic infections.

In patients with non severe TB-IRIS, treatment is based on non-steroidal anti-inflammatory
drugs.

In case of unmasking TB-IRIS, TB treatment should be started immediately.

ART should not be interrupted, except in case of life-threatening IRIS.
