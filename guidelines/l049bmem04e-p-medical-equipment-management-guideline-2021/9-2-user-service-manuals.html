OCP – Operational Centre Paris

MyMSF - An intranet information platform, made and administrated by OCP IT teams. It
gathers all OCP Departmental information and essential documentation. All reference
materials are in the Toolboxes, such as guidelines and protocols. Anyone from MSF can
access with their MSF email and Outlook password. A generic login is also available if you do
not have an MSF email - contact your technical referent for details.

https://mymsf.org/jcms/prod1_16660/fr/logistic-toolbox-wplace

OCG – Operational Centre Geneva

Logistics Referential – A technical support website with navigation by various categories and
a search function. Available also in an offline version with an update function when
connected. Open access, no login required.

www.logistic.ocg.msf.org

Kompas – General support website for all departments, hosted on SharePoint. Access it with
your MSF email address and login.

https://msfintl.sharepoint.com/sites/KompasV2/SitePages/Home.aspx
