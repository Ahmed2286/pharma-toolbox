9 ANNEX I: Internet Resources

This guideline covers the basics for management of medical devices in MSF
projects/missions. Each section has repositories for support documentation, and the
technical working groups are expanding their libraries for support documents that are valid
for all sections. The external sites provide additional or more general information but do not
supersede advice given on the MSF sites.

MSF Support Platforms

MSF International Office / all sections

Medical Portal, hosted on SharePoint. It provides access to all international medical
documents and links to the working group sites for the clinical specialisations. Access with
your MSF email address and login.

https://msfintl.sharepoint.com/sites/msfintlcommunities/medical

Nursing-Care Working Group website, hosted on SharePoint. Access it with your MSF email
address and login.

https://msfintl.sharepoint.com/sites/msfintlcommunities/nccg

MSF International Office / Online catalogue

UNICAT – The online catalogue of items available for ordering through the MSF European
Supply Centres

https://unicat.msf.org/

OCA – Operational Centre Amsterdam

OCA Logistics Expertise Website (LEW) – A technical support website, hosted on SharePoint.
Navigation by technical family and access to tools and documents. Access it with your (any
OC) MSF email address and login.

https://msfintl.sharepoint.com/sites/OCA-DEPT-LOG-Field

OCB – Operational Centre Brussels

Sherlog - A field support platform for OCB. It offers access to several knowledge bases and
discussion platforms. Individual access, request for login can be made via the site.

https://sherlog.msf.org

OCBA – Operational Centre Barcelona & Athens

LogLi - The Logistics Library, known as The LogLi or the Log Field Kit, is the first line of
logistical support you will have in OCBA Field projects. Access it with your (OCBA) MSF email
and login.

https://msfintl.sharepoint.com/sites/OCBA-LogLi/
