Global HIV epidemic statistics

In light of the above information, it is informative to note the status of the epidemic
globally, and particularly in sub-Saharan Africa, where MSF has the bulk of its HIV
projects. The UNAIDS 2020 report notes the following for 2019:

•
There are now 38 million people worldwide infected with HIV, of whom 25.4
million are on treatment.

•
There were 1.7 million new infections, 62% of which are in key populations.

•
There were 690 000 HIV-related deaths.

•
1 in 4 HIV infections in sub-Saharan Africa are adolescent girls and young
women.

•
More than 50% of adults have discriminatory attitudes towards PLHIV.
