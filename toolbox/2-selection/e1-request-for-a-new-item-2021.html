The introduction of a new medical item can be requested by a mission or a medical referent of the HQ

The process of validation is composed of several steps and can be more or less long

To know before

• If the request comes from a mission, it has to be discussed in advance with the Medical Referent of the

HQ and the Medical of the Cell in order to evaluate the relevance of the request.

• Check that the request form is available. It can be find in MyMSF - Medical Toolbox - Pharmacy - Forms.

• Never send a request form directly to MSF Logistique, this will delay the process.
