POINTS TO BE CONSIDERED FOR CHOOSING OR EVALUATING THE UNIT SUPPLY SYSTEM AMONG A PROJECT

Understand the advantages and inconveniences of the different systems.

Understand the weak points of the current system before making any changes.

Knowledge of Human Resources at pharmacy and end user level units (number & skills).

Knowing pharmacies opening hours (project, OPD, etc.).

Knowing the structure size and the activities volume, as well as the specificity of each unit activity.

Knowing the storage conditions and capacities.

Understand patients flow:

o Management of the hospitalized patients after being visited at the emergency room

o Management of transferred or discharged patients

Evaluate accessibility of stock vs. unit (distance, security).

Consider a different supply model for the : Drugs / Medical devices
