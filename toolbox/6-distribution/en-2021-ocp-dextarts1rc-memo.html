MEMO: ARTESUNATE, 100mg, rectal capsules (DEXTARTS1RC) from

Cipla + Strides

Date: 01/07/2021

This product has been supplied to MSF Missions since 2017. The Shelf Life of the Artesunate rectal
capsules is 24 months when stored below 25°C. However, the product has a limited stability at
temperatures exceeding 30°C, therefore utmost care should be taken to avoid excursions above 30°C
during storage and transportation of the product.

Since long term storage conditions below 25oC cannot be guaranteed at the hands of the community
health workers (CHW) the supplier and WHO recommend that the product is handled by CHW, only as
