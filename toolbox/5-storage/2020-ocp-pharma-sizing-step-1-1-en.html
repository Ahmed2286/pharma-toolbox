WARNING: First, define the strategy!

New activity? Suppression of activity?
international orders Chronogram!
Intermediate pharmacy? Transit stock? Unit order chronogram? Etc.

Pharmacist role:

  • Total volume calculation of the medical material:

    • By considering for each item the case where consumption/quantity would be the maximum. See 2020 OCP Step 1 1 Pharma Vol Calculation EN FR

  • Communicate this volume to logistics:

    • They will be the responsible on translating this brut volume of medical articles in absolute need it volume on a pharmacy scale.

Define de Medical articles List:

  • Project MSL updated

To prevent counting the volume of articles no longer in use and to prevent forgetting the volume of articles recently order.

New activities... new articles!

  • Anticipate properly over the next year the new activities and potentially new associated items that will subsequently be added to the MSL

  • If in the long term the project/mission is likely to grow considerably, the percentage of increase in activity must be communicated to the logs.

To choose the appropriate consumption values

  • Average Monthly Consumption (AMC)

    • Reflect as many columns are necessary to cover the maximal AMC for the articles link to seasonal activities and not seasonal ones.

    • Example :

      • 12 last months

      • AMC malaria peak

      • AMC malnutrition peak

      • AMC of last 4 months corresponding to a new activity

  • Forecasted Monthly Consumption (FMC)

    • Stablish the FMC for all the order periods over the year.

    • Attention: take into consideration the needs related to new activities.

    • This FMC will be reflected on the spread sheet « main stock » column H of 2020 OCP Step 1 1 Pharma Vol Calculation EN FR

Which average consumption will choose?

  • The highest!

    • Line by line, for each article, chooses the highest quantity.

    • Afterwards multiply by the cover periodicity and the number of month of security stock

AMCmax X (CP+ SS)= theoritical max quantity

  • See calculations on the spread sheet « main stock » column H of 2020 OCP Step 1 1 Pharma Vol Calculation EN FR

Calculation of volume 

  • MSF Logistique Data

    • With the help of MSF Logistique data, translate the necessary quantity in terms of packaging (round up) and afterwards in volume.

See spread sheet MSFLog201912 on 2020 OCP Step 1 1 Pharma Vol Calculation EN FR

Volume to be communicated to the logistics

  • Volume distribution by category

    • The logs needs knowing the volumes divided into:

      • Psychotropic narcotics, dangerous items, and cold chain items.

      • Later for the rest, what it will be on shelves, over pallets or over racks.

      • Also is advised to estimate the max volume experienced by overstock, sleeping stock and expired items.

      • Finally do not forget considering the prepositioned stocks. See the spread sheet “EPREP” on sheet MSFLog201912 on 2020 OCP Step 1 1 Pharma Vol Calculation EN FR

  • Shelves, palettes and racks.

    • At the end of the table add 3 columns to name « shelves », « palettes » and eventually «racks” See for example the columns “S”, “T” and “V” into spread sheet « main stock » column H of 2020 OCP Step 1 1 Pharma Vol Calculation EN FR

    • For each line place a cross on the appropriate columns.

    • Attention: for some articles (example: paracetamol tablets), a part will be on the shelves and the rest on pallets. Estimate and precise the volume over the shelves and the volume over the palette.

    • Afterwards filter to have the volume by category.

  • Expired

    • A method for the estimation of expired items volume is to analyse the frequency of destruction carried on the mission. Afterwards calculate the maximal volume of expired items (units + main pharmacy) over different periods.

See spread sheet « expired » on 2020 OCP Annex Step 1 1 Pharma Vol Calculation EN FR

See spread sheet TOTAL on 2020 OCP Annex Step 1 1 Pharma Vol Calculation EN FR

And afterwards?

  • The excel file

    • The logistics will translate those volumes into total surfaces for the pharmacy with the help of an standardize excel spread sheet.

See Dimensionnement Pharmacie / Pharmacy sizing available at https://mymsf.org/jcms/15373723_DBFileDocument/fr/dimensionnement-pharmacie-/-pharmacy-sizing

  • The pharmacist must cooperate and follow closely this step.

  • Final schema final of the pharmacy

    • The logisticians will translate the data from the excel spread sheet into schemes with volume and stock organisations.

    • The pharmacist should cooperate and follow this step closely.

Conclusion

  • It’s indispensable to define the strategy in advance.

  • To choose the maximal quantities for each article.

  • Along all the process, the cooperation with all the team in general and in particular with the logistics is the key to the success. The logistics and the medical team are interdependent.
