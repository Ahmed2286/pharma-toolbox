Slide 4: Specification: microbiology

=> Stability studies performed for each product by the manufacturer to confirm the product shelf life and storage conditions
Done at 30±2°C and 40±2°C
Check of:

Organoleptic stability: taste, odour, product consistency and behavior (absence of oil separation, absence of oxidation)
Integrity of the packing materials and marking
Nutritional value and nutrient stability (maintenance of a level of vitamins within specifications during the entire shelf life).
Absence of microbial growth

Storage and transport conditions = temperature < 30°C (or vitamins degradations start)

► Storage < 30°C for all NFOS products

► Transport < 30°C for therapeutic products

(therapeutic milks, RUTF, enteral nutrition,)
► Transport at uncontrolled conditions tolerated for other products
(e.g. emergency food rations)
