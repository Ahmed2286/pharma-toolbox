Preparation sheet isothermal box 16 liters, for

rerouting capital/programs

Maximum Cold Life 48h

Preparation with 9 pre-conditioned ice packs 0.6l Emballiso or

Electrolux (RCW25)

Pre-conditionning: take out the ice packs from the freezer and let
them warm up until there is 5 cm of water in the bottom of the ice

pack

Top part : 1 ice pack

Small side : 2 horizontal ice
packs

Long side : 2 upright ice packs

Warning
Do not re-use this set:
white box & icepacks in
red, usefull only for
international transports

Use original separators or
other cardboard to avoid
direct contact of icepacks
and medical items.
