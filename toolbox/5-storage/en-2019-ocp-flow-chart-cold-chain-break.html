WHAT TO DO IN CASE OF COLD CHAIN BREAK ?

WARNING : THIS NOTICE IS VALID ONLY FOR FIELD BREAKES ;

FOR COLD CHAIN BREAKES DURING INTERNATIONAL TRANSPORT PLEASE FOLLOW THE MSF Logistic PROCEDURE :

  • FOR THE RUPTURES CONCERNING THE BLOOD BANK ADRESS TO THE LABORATORY REFERENTS: complete the blood bank CCB report form and send it to Parislabo@msf.org

PROJECT ►The pharmacy supervisor verifies the situation:

  1. Press the « mark » button of the LogTag to indicate the moment in the logging curve.

  2. Place immediately all the articles in quarantine at the correct range (+2-+8°C) in another medical fridge or in passive cold chain, with the appropriate monitoring tools (logtag and freez tag).

  3. Label clearly « DO NOT USE UNTIL FURTHER NOTICE/ COLD CHAIN BREAK ».

  4. Inform immediately the Project Medical Responsible (PMR), the Logistician and the Project Pharmacy Responsible.

PROJECT as soon as possible, by the Project Pharmacy Responsible:

► Send the standard cold chain break Report by labeling it in the following way YEARMONTHDAY_CCB_COUNTRY_PROJET (20161121_CCB_NG_JAHUN) to the mission pharmacist + the LogTag readings; with the PMR and field Log responsible in cc.

WARNING !

WRITE 1 REPORT PER EQUIPMENT

MISSION PHARMACIST:

► Verifies report for its completeness and asks the project for clarification if needed.

► Once the report is complete, s/he sends the report and the LogTag reading to parispharma@paris.msf.org and [personal contact removed — see the original document in the Toolbox]

► WAIT FOR THE RESPONSE FROM THE HQ.

► Proposes corrective actions with the support of the LogCo if necessary.

► DO NOT USE

► OK TO USE

► OK TO USE BUT WITH RESTRICTIONS

PROJECT: Project Pharmacy Responsible.

► Take the products out of quarantine cold chain and store them in the expired/damaged area for future destruction.

► Register the « OUT » on the stock card of each product and note « cold chain break » on the « comment » section.

► Register the « OUT expired/damaged » in Isystock and note « cold chain break » in the « comment » section.

PROJECT: Project Pharmacy Responsible.

► Indicate clearly the date of the cold chain break on the box of all the products that were affected by the break.

► Take the products out of quarantine and put them back into the normal circuit (with or without restriction) keeping the monitoring tools with them if possible.

► Clearly note the restriction on the product box and on the stock card (ex. new expiration date) + make a partial inventory if there is an expiration date modification.

PROJET ► ONLY IF NEEDED, make a loan / donation request or place a complementary order, after checking back orders, to replace the products.

PROJET (lessons learnt): Project Pharmacy Responsible in collaboration with the logistic team:

► Analyze the causes of the cold chain break.

► Take the appropriate measures to prevent that the problem happens again in the future.

► Make sure that the personnel is trained on the good management practices of the cold chain.

► Review the cold chain contingency plan with the mission and the project - if it didn’t work as planned.
